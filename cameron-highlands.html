Cameron Highlands

35 interesting places to visit — a cool & relaxing holiday destination in Pahang with tea plantations, lush gardens, strawberry farms, and stunning nature trails.

Why Visit?

Cameron Highlands is a dream destination for those seeking a cool and relaxing holiday surrounded by nature. Imagine vast green hills, fresh cool air, and endless tea plantations. Who says paradise on earth doesn't exist!

As you explore this highland paradise, you'll discover charming small towns, each with its own unique atmosphere. There are also plenty of fun activities awaiting your whole family — from exploring the largest tea plantations in Malaysia to picking and tasting fresh strawberries straight from the plant.

Whether you're a nature lover, a foodie, or simply looking for a place to escape the hustle and bustle of city life, Cameron Highlands has something special for you.

Quick Facts

  • Best for: Nature, family trips, and photography
  • Temperature: Around 15°C to 24°C
  • Recommended stay: 2 to 3 days
  • Top food: Steamboat, fresh strawberries, and corn
  • Location: Pahang, Malaysia
  • Total attractions: 35+

35 Interesting Places in Cameron Highlands

Gardens & Flora

Cameron Highlands Flora Park

Surrounded by the greenery of Kea Farm, the Flora Park showcases a beautiful landscape filled with colourful flora, just steps away from the Kea Farm Market.

Cactus Point

One of the oldest attractions in Cameron Highlands and an ideal destination for cactus lovers. Located between Kea Farm and Brinchang town, it sells a variety of plants, flowers, and cacti at affordable prices.

O&R Garden

Located at Kea Farm between Copthorne Cameron Highlands and Cameron Square. Imagine walking among more than 100 types of orchids and 30 types of roses in full bloom.

Cactus Valley

Rarely do we get to visit a garden filled with various types of cacti. At Cactus Valley, you can enjoy walking through a beautiful garden adorned with diverse cactus species.

Lavender Garden

One of the most enchanting places in Cameron Highlands. It feels like stepping into a purple paradise, with lavender in every corner as far as the eye can see.

Tan's Camellia Garden

A hidden garden built by its owner, Madam Tan and her husband, who have a background in botany. They transformed an English-style bungalow into an amazing garden with over 200 plant species including lilies, azaleas, gardenias, magnolias, wild orchids, and herb trees.

Rose Valley

If you're looking for a great photo spot in Cameron Highlands, Rose Valley is the perfect choice. This vast rose garden is home to over 450 rose varieties — you might feel that even 2 hours isn't enough to explore it all!

Green View Garden

An interesting destination in Kampung Raja where you can see various vegetables grown hydroponically, strawberry gardens, and more.

Tea Plantations

BOH Tea Plantation, Sungai Palas

You drink BOH tea every day at home but have never seen the plantation? Want to know how your daily tea is made? Visit the BOH Tea Plantation located in Sungai Palas.

Cameron Valley Tea House 1 & 2

Cameron Valley Tea House 1 and Cameron Valley Tea House 2 are two of three tea plantations operated by Bharat Tea Plantation.

Cameron Valley Tea House, Kuala Terla

One of three tea houses owned by the Bharat Group, located in Kuala Terla in the northern part of Cameron Highlands. Less visited compared to others due to its more remote location, making it a peaceful retreat.

Strawberry Farms

KHM Strawberries & Jam

A local Bumiputera-owned strawberry farm where visitors can tour the gardens and witness the entire strawberry growing process — from seed to fruit — and even pick strawberries themselves.

YZ Agro Farm (Strawberry Farm & Café)

A Muslim-owned strawberry farm in Tanah Rata. What makes this farm unique? Not only do they grow strawberries, they also serve tempoyak patin — a rare combination you won't find elsewhere!

Big Red Strawberry Farm

Want to enjoy the freshness of just-picked strawberries? At Big Red Strawberry Farm, you can taste fresh strawberries and witness the strawberry growing process. Most strawberries in Malaysia come from Cameron Highlands.

Raju Hill Strawberry Farm

Located just 3 km from Brinchang, this strawberry farm offers two interesting attractions at the same location.

Animal Attractions

Animal Feeding Rainbow Garden

Kids love watching the antics of cute little animals? Head to this garden where you can meet rabbits, peacocks, guinea pigs, turtles, goats, and alpacas.

The Sheep Sanctuary

Want to see the funny antics of cute fluffy Merino sheep? Then head to The Sheep Sanctuary in Cameron Highlands for an adorable farm experience.

Highlands Apiary Farm

A popular bee farm in Cameron located in Ringlet, near the BOH Tea Plantation. It is managed by the same operation as the honey stalls at Kea Farm and Brinchang night market.

ZooMania Butterfly Farm

If you're interested in learning about the natural life cycle of butterflies or want to see various beautiful butterfly species, visit Zoomania De' Butterfly Farm in Brinchang.

Ee Feng Gu Bee Farm

A must-visit near Kea Farm, not far from Brinchang town centre. Learn about the honey-making process and proper bee-keeping techniques. The place is arranged like a maze, adding to the fun of walking around.

Nature & Adventure

Lata Iskandar Waterfall

On the way to Cameron Highlands, 25 kilometres before reaching Ringlet town, you'll find this popular waterfall known as Lata Iskandar.

Robinson Waterfall

The trail to Robinson Waterfall starts from the MARDI Agrotechnology Park and offers a beautiful path leading to the waterfall.

Mossy Forest

Imagine a forest that has existed since the age of dinosaurs. That's the Mossy Forest, where time seems to stand still. It may be a bit far to get here, but if you love hiking or jungle trekking, it's absolutely worth it.

Mount Irau

A top destination for outdoor enthusiasts visiting Cameron Highlands. Thick fog and large trees with sprawling roots create a mysterious atmosphere that's hard to put into words.

Cameron Adventurous

For adventure enthusiasts, there are various options suitable for everyone. ATV activities are open only to individuals aged 16 and above, while 4x4 Safari activities are open to all ages.

Markets & Shopping

Bazaar Kea Farm

If there's still room in your vehicle, don't rush home! Visit Bazaar Kea Farm, located just 2 to 3 km from Brinchang and Tringkap — an area long known for its unrivalled fresh produce.

Arked Peladang Cameron Highlands

Want to add to your home garden plant collection? You've come to the right place — here you can bring home seedlings that come directly from the farm.

Brinchang Night Market

The Cameron Highlands Night Market has been relocated from Brinchang town to Sungai Ruil, near Cameron Golden Hills Resort. Arrive early to avoid parking difficulties, or stay at a hotel nearby.

Museums, Culture & Education

Coronation Park (Taman Pertabalan)

Want to take your family for a stroll without spending a cent? This free attraction is located in the town centre of Tanah Rata — perfect for enjoying fresh air and admiring the beautiful surrounding landscape.

MARDI Agrotechnology Park

A renowned destination in Cameron Highlands that successfully attracts around 65,000 visitors annually. It is a MARDI research centre in Malaysia dedicated to agricultural innovation.

Time Tunnel Museum

Opened in 2007, this museum is filled with treasures from the past. It was established by Mr. See Kok Shan, a passionate antique collector.

Mah Meri Art Gallery

A must-visit for art and history enthusiasts. Located in Tanah Rata, this gallery showcases the unique talent of the Mah Meri community, known for their extraordinary wood carving skills.

Food & Cafés

Avant Chocolate

Love sweets? Avant Chocolate is a dream destination for dessert lovers. Don't be mistaken — this place is not just an ordinary café.

200 Seeds Café

Missing Malay food? Visit 200 Seeds Café for a diverse menu. Being in Cameron Highlands means many dishes feature strawberry elements. A must-try is the nasi lemak with strawberry sambal — truly extraordinary!

KC Kwang & Sons Grape Farm

If you love grapes and farm life, you'll enjoy KC Kwang & Sons (Twin Diamond Farm) in Blue Valley, Kampung Raja. They grow large, sweet Kyoho grapes.

Accommodation
