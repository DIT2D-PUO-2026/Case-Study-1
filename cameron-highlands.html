Cameron Highlands

A refreshing hill station in Pahang known for cool temperatures, beautiful tea terraces, and peaceful nature trails.

Why Visit?

Cameron Highlands offers one of the most relaxing mountain escapes in Malaysia. The weather is usually cooler than the lowlands, making it perfect for slow travel and outdoor activities.

Popular attractions include BOH Tea Plantation, Mossy Forest, strawberry farms, and local night markets where you can find fresh produce and handmade snacks.

Quick Facts

  • Best for: Nature, family trips, and photography
  • Temperature: Around 15C to 24C
  • Recommended stay: 2 to 3 days
  • Top food: Steamboat, fresh strawberries, and corn

Suggested Itinerary (1 Day)

Morning

Visit BOH Tea Plantation and enjoy fresh tea with hill views.

Afternoon

Explore strawberry farms and local flower gardens.

Evening

Walk through the night market and try local street food.
